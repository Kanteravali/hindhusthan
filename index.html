About Us

Hindustan Scouts and Guides Tribal Welfare Association is a voluntary, non-political, educational movement for young people. We combine the spirit of Scouting and Guiding with the mission of Tribal Welfare, working towards the holistic development of youth and marginalized communities across India.

Vision

To build a society where every child and young person develops leadership, discipline, and a spirit of service, while ensuring the upliftment of tribal communities through education, skill training, and welfare.

Mission

To nurture youth into responsible citizens through Scouting and Guiding. Support tribal communities in education, healthcare, and livelihood. Create awareness on environment, health, and social responsibility.
